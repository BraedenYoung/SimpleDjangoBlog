So, for Halloween my girlfriend and I were planning on dressing up as Doctor Who, and the TARDIS. Unfortunately, when we received the light-up headband the light didn't fade in and out.



To fix this I made a pretty well documented circuit using a 555 timer, a capacitor, and a transistor. Due to a bit lack of time and parts, I needed to slap three resistors in serial to get the required resistance needed to control the fading of the light.


The final circuit ended up coming out but really pushing the space restrictions of the headband. This resulted in having to remove the internal battery for an external battery that I figured could easily be tucked in the back of a dress.


It also required using a rotary hand tool to clear out as much as I could from the housing.
